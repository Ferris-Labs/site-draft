Founding Ferris Labs AG

Ferris Labs AG was founded on 1.1.2021 in Zug, Switzerland by a group of Data and Technology enterpreneurs, each bringing many years of successful track records in international and corporate business.

The Ferris platform has been developed and established at prime customer sights since 2016. The reason for founding Ferris Labs was to establish Ferris as an industry leading data and process platform across industries and geographies.

Ferris Labs is an indepependent and privately owned company. The organic growth of Ferris ensures laser sharp focus on development, distribution and growth.

Evolution and History

The Ferris technology ecosystem began its journey in 2016 when the first Big Data and AI platform for a leading global Private and Wealth Management Banks in Switzerland was developed and went live by year-end. Today, the Ferris Platform forms the core of the banks global data management strategy, sourcing approximately 75% of its global data and serving dozens of analytics and AI use cases.

From the beginning, key architectural principles of Ferris were set, based on open source technologies and a hybrid cloud approach.

Meanwhile, Ferris has evolved into an open and integrative platform, working on a minimum set of hardware and fully hybrid cloud enabled. The templated deployment model further enables a tailored setup, ensuring that only those components are used that serve customers needs.

“One of our learnings of the past years has been that a Data Strategy should never stand alone. It must include a strong Application and Process focus in order to ensure the optimal integration with the organisations business and technology endeavours.”

The need for Agile development and fast time to market of projects, paired with an inherent challenge to harness ever more complex technologies, have lead Ferris to become more and more events and services driven. Sourcing new data and ingesting services fast has thus become the logical baseline of Ferris.

All of this has driven the evolution of Ferris to divert our research and development outside of the sole scope of Big Data and into the space of AI/ML, DevOps, Enterprise Automation and Operations.

Together with our customers, partners and advisors we continuously push the envelope of technology, ensuring Ferris stays at the forefront of DeepTech!
