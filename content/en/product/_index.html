Ferris DX

Ferris Digital Transformation (DX) is the platform for building highly scalable, cross-network sync or async microservices and agents.

Our flagship product for rapidly building multi-cloud event driven microservices. Accelerate your digital transformation by linking hordes of micro-integrations with zero deployment and zero downtime.

In short, enables the fast to-market of new interconnected applications as well as the effortless migration of old legacy systems onto an agile and future proof infrastructure.

Ferris DSM

Ferris Data and Service Mesh (DSM) equals Data Platform as a Service.

Infrastructure and services to support your digital transformation and augment your existing infrastructure. Leverage our tried and tested reference architecture to build your custom Data Lake and Processing environment.

A fast growing ecosystem of source data adaptors and pre-built micro-services support a fast learning curve, agility and reusability of assets as well as an even faster time-to-market.

Ferris SX

Coming soon. Ferris Streaming Automation (SX), is our latest addition to the state-of-the-art DX suite.

A radically simplified approach to creating and deploying stream processing for 100s of Apache Kafka streams. Reduce the time to build stream processing applications by a factor of 10X or more.

Automate the way you source, stream and manage data and dramatically reduce the impact on topics management, DevOps and DataOps.
